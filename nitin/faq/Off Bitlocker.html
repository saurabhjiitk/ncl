How to save the BitLocker key in case BitLocker is ON


Step 1:Right click on BitLocker encrypted drive and select “Manage BitLocker”.



Step 2:We have to set a password on the BitLocker encrypted drive, for that click on “Add password”



Step 3: Now you are required to specify a password for encrypting the drive, enter the password and click "Finish". You should choose a password having a combination of upper & lower-case letters, numbers, spaces, and special symbols. Once the encryption is complete, you can access the drive using this password.



Step 4: In the BitLocker Drive Encryption control panel for regenerating a copy of recovery key select “Back up your recovery key”.



Step 5: For saving BitLocker recovery key we have 4 options. Choose the appropriate option to save your recovery key save to a file, USB flash drive, or printing the key are the simplest method. Please note that anyone can use the recovery key to gain access to the drive, even if they don’t know the unlock password of your encrypted drive, so please do not disclose it to others.



1)Save to a USB flash drive: First we have to insert a USB flash drive in our PC, after that click on “Save to a USB flash drive”.



Now select the USB device from the list and click on “Save”.



A copy of BitLocker recovery key will be saved on your USB device as shown in below example fig.



2)Save to a file: For saving the recovery key as a .txt file on local drive select “Save to a file”.



Click on Save button to save the recovery key on your local disk as shown below.



3)Print the recovery key: To print the recovery key, select “Print the recovery key”.



Select the printer from the list and hit Print button to print the recovery key.
