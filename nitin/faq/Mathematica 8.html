Installation of Mathematica 8 in Microsoft Windows

Step 1:Download the setup file of Mathematica 8 frome CC FTP site, after downloading extract the zipped setup file to computer Local Drive. Open the extracted folder and double click on the Mathematica setup.exe file to install software on your computer.


Step 2:Click on the "Next" button as shown in below fig.



Step 3:Click on the "Next" button.



Step 4:Select "Install all components(recommended)" options and click on the "Next" button as shown in below fig.



Step 5:Click on the "Next" button.



Step 6:Click on the "Next" button.



Step 7:Wait for 5 - 10 minutes to complete the installation process.



Step 8:Now open the “Mathematica 8” software and wait for initialization. “After a few second “Wolfram product Activation” window apairs. Choose “Other ways to activate” to activate Mathematica8 License.



Step 9:Click on “Connect to a network license server” option.



Step 10:Enter the “Network License Server” IP in the Server name box (License server is 172.31.1.4 or 172.31.1.5 or akash2.cc.iitk.ac.in).and click on “Activate”.



Step 11:Check the box “I accept the terms of this agreement” and Click on “OK” button.
