Moving BitLocker encrypted drive to another Windows computer


When we move BitLocker encrypted HDD to another Windows PC, your drives protected with BitLocker are identified with a lock icon in Windows Explorer as shown below in figure Local Disk (F:) is with closed lock icon. We can unlock protected drive with the help of BitLocker encryption drive password or BitLocker saved recovery key.


1)Unlocking drive with password:


Step 1:Double click on the locked drive to open it.



Step 2:Enter the drive password.



Step 3:Select “Open folder to view files” to open the drive-in windows explorer.


2)Unlocking drive with recovery key: If you forgot your password then you can unlock protected drive with the help of earlier saved BitLocker recovery key.


Step 1:Double click on the locked drive to open it.



Step 2:Click on “More options” in pop up window as shown in below figure.



Step 3:Select “Enter recovery key” in pop up window as shown in below figure.



Step 4:Now check the recovery key file “BitLocker Recovery key.txt” for recovery key, like shown below figure.



Step 5:Now enter 48-digit recovery key in pop up window and click on “Unlock”.



Step 6:Select “Open folder to view files” to open the drive-in windows explorer.
