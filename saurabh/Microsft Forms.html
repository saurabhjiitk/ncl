Microsoft Forms

Microsoft Forms is a web-based application within an Office 365 suite that allows users to build quick intake forms and take surveys.

You can easily create surveys, quizzes, and polls and share with your friends, colleague and in institute to take their opinion on a particular topic or get their details for data. You can add attractive existing theme to your form.

---How to use Google Forms---
(All the steps are highlighted with red box)

1. Go to Microsoft 365 site and login with your Microsoft Account and Password
Microsoft 365 Site

2. If you logged in successfully then you will find this screen layout then click on Forms

3. This is the Forms layout Here you can create New Form or New Quiz. Click on that option which you want to create

4. If you want to create a form you will find many option to add in your form…You can add
I. Choice
II. Text
III. Rating
IV. Date
V. Ranking
VI. Section etc.

5. Once you completed your form creation you can share it with your colleague in your department or share with anyone…
