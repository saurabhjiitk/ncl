Autodesk Educational Account

Step:-1 Go to Autodesk Educational website with following link   Autodesk Educational Site

Step:-2 Now click on Sign In you will get following window and click on CREATE ACCOUNT

Step:-3 Create your educational account.

Step:-4 Now login with your register ID and Password

Step:-5 You will get this window and click on GET STARTED

Step:-6 Now complete your educational profile

Step:-7 Now upload your ID to complete the process

Step:-8 After 48 Hours your account will verify from Autodesk. After verified you can download and install any Autodesk product.
