About FMS (Facility Management Service)


Role

The role of FMS is to operate and maintain the labs, provide technical help to users/students regarding usage and keep the lab-ready to use for the users/students for the entire period of operation.

About CC-01 and CC-03 Windows Laboratory

CC-01 and CC-03 Windows Labs are located on the 3rd floor of the New Core Building. This lab is belonging to Computer Center.

Each lab has 55 computers with an Intel Core i7 processor with 16 GB RAM and a 1 TB SSD Hard Disk.

These labs are not available from 01:30 AM to 08:30 AM every day.

The computer center has presently two windows labs in New Core Building. These labs are used by IITK Community for academic courses, Mid/end Semester examinations, workshops, TeQIP, and institute placement exams.


Lab Booking:-

IITK academic teaching staff members (faculty) can book this lab using https://booklab.cc.iitk.ac.in/iit/login.pl

Computer center user name and password can be used for logging in to the interface.
