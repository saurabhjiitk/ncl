*~ Syllabus for half-yearly exam ~*

ENGLISH:

  • Capital letters (A- Z)
  • Small letters in cursive (a –z)
  • Vowel sound words:-
    • ‘a’- mat, cat, rat, van, pan, fan
    • ‘e’- pen, hen, ten, net, egg, bell
  • Colours- red, blue, green, pink
  • Oral:
    • Rhymes-
      • One two three
      • Ding dong bell
      • Humpty Dumpty
      • Cock crows
    • Seven days of a week

MATHS:

  • Numbers: 1-60
  • What comes after (1- 30)
  • What comes before (1- 20)
  • What comes in between (1- 30)
  • Backward counting (20- 1)
  • Complete the series (1- 40)
  • Shapes - square, circle, rectangle, triangle, cone and oval.
  • Circle the bigger and smaller number (1-10)

SCIENCE (Oral & Drawing):

  • Good and bad habits
  • Living and non-living things
  • Concept of hot and cold
  • Sources of water
  • Uses of water
  • Concept of rainbow
