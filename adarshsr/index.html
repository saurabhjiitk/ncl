How to Request files via OneDrive Step by Step


Step 1 : MS office 365 login with your "User Id" and "Password". Then click on All apps icon as shown in circle and
Then click on "OneDrive" app.




Step 2 : And then create a new folder named as "Project files" (or any other name as you wish) by clicking on "New" as shown below.




Step 3 : Then Click on "Folder".




Step 4 : By clicking on "Project files" several features will appear on the top.




Step 5 : Then click on "Request files" .




Step 6 : After clicking "Request files" In the edit box "What files are you requesting?" type some description like "TA101 Project Files" .




Step 7 : After click on "Next" . button. Copy the URL as shown in below image..




Step 8 : After copying the URL , Open any mail software like Thunderbird, Outlook or Webmail and Click on "Insert/edit link" button.




Step 9 : Then paste URL link which you have copied from Step 7 in "URL" box and Type text in "Text to display"
box eg. "please upload files here" to display the text. Then enter the descriptive email and Sent it.




Step 10 : Recipient will get a request mail which you had sent. Recipient will have to Click on "Please upload files here" link.




Step 11 : Recipient will get a message like shown below, after that click on "Select files" . and select all file which you want to upload.
Recipient can add one or multiple files at a time.




Step 12 : After selecting all Project files , enter "First name" and "Last name" . and click on "Upload" .
After uploading the Project files message will appear that files are successfully uploaded.




Final step : After uploading the Project files by Recipient you will get those request files as shown in below image.
