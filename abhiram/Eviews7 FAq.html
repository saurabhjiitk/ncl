How to Install EViews 7 in Microsoft Window 10

1. Run the EViews7 setup.exe. And Click on "Install EViews" button.

2. Click on the "Next" button.

3. Read License Agreement carefully and then Click on "Yes" button.

4. Choose the Destination Location and Click on "Next" button.

5. Enter Serial Number and Click on "Next" button.

6. Enter the cloning Key and Click on "Next" button

7. Enter the License Server IP and Port Number. The License Server IP is "172.31.1.147"and Port No. is 25750.

8. Again enter License server IP & Port Number. Click on "Next" buton.

9. Check "Select Components" and Click on the "Next" button.

10. Select Program Folder and Click on the "Next" Button.

11. To ask question .click on the "Yes"

12. To Ask Question "Would you like to create an icon for Eviews on your desktop" and Click on the "Yes" Button.

13. Eview Successfully installed click on "finish" buton.
