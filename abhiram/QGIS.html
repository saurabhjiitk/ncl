How to Install QGIS in Microsoft Window 10

1. Run the Setup “QGIS-OSGeo4w-3.4.6-2-Setup-x86.exe” and Click on “Next” Button. .

2. Accept the license agreement click on the “I Agree” Button.

3. “Choose Install Location” (keep it to default) and click on “Next&38221; button.

4. “Choose the Components” as users requirement and Click on the “Install” button.

5. The Installer will proceed to complete the installation. Wait a few minute.

6. After completing the installation. Click on the “Reboot Now” Button.
