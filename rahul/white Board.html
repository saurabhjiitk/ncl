How to use whiteboard


What is whiteboard?

* Microsoft Whiteboard is a freeform, digital canvas where people, content, and ideas come together.
You can use Whiteboard for collaborating with your team to accomplish many activities. whether your team is in the same place or in multiple locations.Team members can work collaboratively using their own devices.


How to use whiteboard in your browser?


* First of all you have to login with your Microsoft account. Write your email and type your Microsoft account password.






* After successfully login you will go to All apps option and select whiteboard option then click it.






* Go to whiteboard and click Create new whiteboard option, then open it.






* Click the Pen button , and then start writing or drawing in black, or click the down arrow next to the button, and then select another pen color to draw with.
